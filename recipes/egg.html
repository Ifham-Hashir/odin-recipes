Fried Egg

The absolute best fried egg with crispy, golden edges and a perfectly runny yolk for sunny side
up or medium-hard yolk for over easy.

Ingredients

  • 1 large fresh egg
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • Flaky sea salt , for serving
  • Freshly ground black pepper , for serving

Steps

  1. Crack an egg in a small ramekin and place it near the stove.
  2. Warm an 8-inch cast iron skillet over medium-high heat until
    its hot enough that a drop of water sizzles rapidly on contact.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add in the butter, moving it around to coat the pan.
  4. Gently pour the egg into the center of the pan (and watch out for splatters.)
  5. Let the egg cook until the edges are crisp and golden and egg white is opaque,
    but the yolk is still runny, 2 to 2 1/2 minutes.
  6. Transfer the cooked egg to a plate.Sprinkle with flaky sea salt and a couple
    turns of freshly ground black pepper, if desired.
