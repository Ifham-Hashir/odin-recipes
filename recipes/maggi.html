Italian Maggi

Loaded with Italian Flavours, this recipe packs a punch and I am sure you are going to love this
mélange of Maggi Flavour combined with the slightly sweet and tangy Italian touch to it.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil 1 tbsp
  • Garlic 1 teaspoon (Chopped)
  • Onion ¼ cup (Chopped
  • Mushroom 2-3 (Cut into pieces)
  • Olives 2 tbsp
  • Pasta sauce 1 tbsp
  • Oregano 1 tsp
  • Maggi Masala 1 packet
  • Parmesan Cheese 1 teaspoon (Grated)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add garlic and onion and fry for a few seconds.
  3. Add mushroom and olives and fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add pasta sauce and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add oregano and Maggi Noodles along with the taste maker.
  6. Add 1 cup of water.
  7. Cook until Maggi is done.
  8. Garnish with grated Parmesan Cheese.
  9. Serve hot.
